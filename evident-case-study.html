Executive Summary

A modern foundation for safety-critical data workflows.

NDT teams rely on fast, accurate data transfer. The previous Evident Connect app felt outdated, cluttered, and too complex for engineers working in high-pressure environments.

I led the product redesign across research, IA, UX, and interface modernization—shifting the platform toward a cleaner, mobile-first experience engineers could trust.

05

Research Insights

Field-driven design shaped by real engineering workflows.

To guide decisions, I conducted focused sessions with engineers across multiple industries. Three insights stood out:

"We don't need more features. We need less noise."
Insight 01

Minimal Interfaces

Engineers want minimal interfaces because their work is already demanding. Every extra element competes for attention they can't spare.

Insight 02

Personalization Required

Shared workspaces cause confusion and require personalization. Different roles need different views of the same data.

Insight 03

Mobile Constraints

Mobile interactions must accommodate limited precision and fast movement. Touch targets matter more than aesthetics.

Research confirmed that simplicity, clarity, and personalization were not preferences but essential requirements.

06

Design Decisions

Reducing cognitive load and restoring control.

Simplicity First

Reduce visual noise and decision fatigue at every touchpoint

Personalized Workspaces

Replace shared environments with role-specific views

Clearer Document Hierarchy

Highlight what matters most through visual weight and spacing

Direct Sorting

Replace complicated filtering with simple, direct controls

Unified Navigation

Create a consistent path through the entire application

Every choice served the same goal: reduce friction and help engineers focus on their actual work.
